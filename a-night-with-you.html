sunday evening · 10 years later

10 years from now…

we're home.

scroll slowly ↓

you're on your laptop…

working, focused like always.

I'm pretending to let you work.

(I'm not.)

I keep stealing your attention

and you act annoyed.

you're not.

you made food today.

of course you won't let me help properly.

"just sit, I've got it"

and then you end up feeding me anyway.

every single time.

(also it's amazing and I won't admit it in the moment)

it's late.

the only light is from the fridge.

we're standing there, half asleep, half laughing

about nothing important.

and then somehow we start dancing.

like idiots.

my favourite kind.

we finally slow down.

just… existing next to each other.

no rush.

no distance.

no countdowns.

just us.

I don't know exactly how life will look.

the city, the apartment, the routines—

all of that, I can figure out.

but I know this part stays.

you making food and not letting me help.

me distracting you from your laptop.

us, at midnight, in fridge light, dancing like idiots.

you… and me… like this.

I already see this life with you, Suchir.

I think I always have.

so come back soon, okay?

we've got a whole life to get to.

— your Golu 🌸
